About Me!


I graduated from VLACS in June of 2020 with the NH
Scholars Program. I temporarily worked at Keypoint
Partners in 2021 to assist with managing the transfer of
files to an online format. Over the years, I have volunteered
at many organizations focused on providing for those in
need, such as the Merrimack Valley Baptist Church Prison
Ministry, the Canine Commitment of New England, and
Vacation Bible School.

Currently, while I still engage in volunteer activities, I
primarily work for pet owners to provide care for animals.
I employ enthusiasm, compassion, and observation skills
in all my work. If you wish to inquire about any of my
jobs, please view my resume by clicking on the button
to the right.

If you wish to know more about me, check out my youtube channel or join the discord!
