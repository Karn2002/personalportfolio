ADHD

My fascination with ADHD has grown in the last few years. As one of the most
predominant disorders out there, I believe it is important to understand it and
find ways to help with the impairments that come with it. I would also like to
see more material that teaches how to use the gifts that ADHD gives.

Autism

Autism is prevalent in my family. Much of my family has ASD, and while I personally
don't know what Autism itself is like, I do understand how it affects those around
the individual. I believe Autism, much like ADHD, can create profoundly intelligent
people if they're taught how to use their gifts instead of fighting them.
